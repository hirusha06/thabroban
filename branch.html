EKHO Ella

EKHO Ella is located in close proximity to popular tourist attractions, hiking trails and many more. In addition to that, EKHO Ella claims to posses the best view of the " Ella Gap".The destination enjoys 23C -30C weather conditions all year round. Ella which also means waterfall in sinhalese: the native language of Sri Lanka, is adorned by several stunning waterfalls amidst lush green mountains.

Oak Ray Wild Yala

A premier among Yala hotels, Oak Ray Wild Yala is situated at Tissamaharama, in close proximity to the Yala wildlife sanctuary. The hotel consists of 44 Superior Deluxe Rooms with modern amenities. The elegant restaurant and bar offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape garden. The pool secluded amongst the landscape garden is a vantage point to soak in spectacular views of the sunset.
